Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is impressle?

impressle is a unique social network where you have to draw by hand, not uploading image files. Follow your favorite artists, give likes to other users' pictures and make your own ones!

How to become an artist on impressle?

  1. Log in to your account (or sign up if you do not have one)
  2. Go to Create Picture page
  3. Draw something impressive
  4. Click "Publish" button
  5. You are now officially a new artist!

What is "Rank"?

User Rank shows their skills at drawing on impressle. More XP -> higher Rank
And if you want to know the details:

0 XP = "Newbie" Rank
1-24 XP = "Artist" Rank
25-49 = "Amateur Artist" Rank
50-99 = "Cool Guy" Rank
100-999 = "Skilled" Rank
1000+ = "impressive" Rank

Also, there are some *special* Ranks. For example: "Admin" is a special Rank and regular users cannot get this Rank

What is "XP"?

As said above, more XP -> higher Rank

You get +1 XP for publishing a picture
You get +1 XP for every follower
You get +1 XP for every like on your picture
You get +1 XP for every comment on your picture
You get -1 XP for deleting a picture
You get -1 XP if your picture gets banned
You get -1 XP if your comment gets banned
You get -1 XP if someone deletes their comment on your picture

You should get more XP to become the best on impressle

Are there any limits on posting pictures and other things?

No! You can post as many pictures as you want without any limits, but don't spam, please

What if my account/picture/comment gets banned?

If your account gets banned, you will not be able to log in to your account anymore and other users will see "[ BANNED ]" tag in your profile

If your picture gets banned, it will be not showing at home page, but users still can view the picture in your profile

If your comment gets banned, its text will be replaced with "[ BANNED ]"

Just don't be a bad guy and we will not ban you :)

Can I trust this website?

Absolutely. Source code of impressle is available on GitHub. You can check it on this repository

I didn't find answer to my question here. Where to ask?

If you have some questions, you can ask on official impressle Telegram community chat. Currently, there are available English and Russian channels and groups. All of them are listed here
Also, you can ask on GitHub (impressle repository -> Issues -> New issue)
